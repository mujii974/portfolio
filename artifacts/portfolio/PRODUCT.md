# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Mixed audience — recruiters evaluating fit, potential clients assessing capability, and peers exploring technical depth. They are on desktop/laptop in a professional context, scanning for signal quickly. Success means the portfolio itself proves the creator's design and engineering quality on first glance.

## Product Purpose

A personal showcase that demonstrates professional capability through its own execution. Every interaction is proof of craft.

## Brand Personality

Precise, confident, restrained. Direct voice, no filler, no hype. The emotional goal: within seconds the visitor thinks, "this person knows what they're doing."

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages with blue gradients and glass cards
- Stock dark-mode templates with particle-network hero backgrounds
- Monospace used as lazy "technical" shorthand
- Stock illustration avatars or generic 3D blobs
- Loud, competing animations and scroll-jacking

## Design Principles

- **Practice what you preach.** The site's own design is the strongest signal of ability.
- **Show, don't tell.** Let the interface demonstrate skill rather than announce it.
- **Expert confidence.** Sparse, no decoration without purpose, generous whitespace.
- **One motion at a time.** Restrained animation guides attention; it does not compete for it.
- **Consistency is discipline.** Type scale, spacing, and rhythm should feel authored, not assembled.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA default. Dark theme with careful contrast for code samples and long-form text. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
