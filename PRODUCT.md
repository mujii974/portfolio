# PRODUCT.md — mujii.dev

register: brand

## What this is
Personal portfolio of Mujtaba Shahid ("Mujii") — Data & Cybersecurity graduate (UDST, Doha, class of 2026), completing CEH v13. The site is the product: its craft is the proof of the "secure development + design" claim. Single-page scroll + 404 + admin route.

## Users
- Recruiters and hiring managers (Qatar + remote security/dev roles) arriving from CV/LinkedIn. They skim in 60 seconds; they need role, location, availability, proof of work, and a CV download without friction.
- Security leads / engineers who will actually dig into the three GitHub projects.
- Agency/freelance clients who care that he ships polished frontend work.

## Brand voice
Three words: **engineered, composed, quietly playful.**
A security operator's precision (mono labels, statuses, dossier framing) with one wink: the chibi "agent Mujii" character in a grey suit. Tagline that survives from the old site: "Precision over noise."

## Identity anchors (carry over, non-negotiable)
- The ASCII portrait canvas in the hero (scramble-in reveal + cursor bulge). It IS the brand mark.
- Electric blue accent family (rgb 79,142,247 dark / deep cobalt light) — the ASCII art is tinted with it.
- JetBrains Mono as the technical voice.
- The chibi character assets (ChatGPT image 2.0, grey double-breasted suit): 5 transparent poses + 1 full-body on navy.

## Anti-references (never)
- Neon-green Matrix/hacker-terminal cosplay. Skulls, padlocks, binary rain.
- Generic AI landing slop: centered hero + 3 icon cards + purple gradients.
- Template portfolio energy (Bootstrap cards, avatar circle, progress-bar skill meters).
- Corporate security-vendor stock art (glowing shields, hooded figures).

## Strategic principles
- Dark-first cinematic; light mode is a true daytime "paper dossier" reading, not an inversion afterthought.
- Every decorative system must be theme-aware, reduced-motion-safe, and cheap on mobile.
- Playfulness is rationed: the character appears at deliberate moments (about card, contact, 404), never as confetti.
- Content is already strong and real — never fabricate projects, numbers, or endorsements.
